---

## Where I Chose Real Images Over AI

I used real screenshots for all of my projects because they provide authentic proof of my work. I also chose a real profile photo because it represents me accurately. AI-generated images were only used for decorative elements, such as the logo and background texture.

---

## Rejected AI Image

I generated a colorful futuristic AI background but decided not to use it because it was too visually distracting. It took attention away from the portfolio content and did not match the clean, professional style of my identity kit.

---

## Design Consistency

All AI-generated visuals follow a clean, minimal, modern style using blue and white colors to match my identity kit.
